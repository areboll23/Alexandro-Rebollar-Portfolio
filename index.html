FEA · Failure Analysis · Standards
Trail King Outrigger Mount — Structural Failure Analysis
What
Root-cause investigation of catastrophic recurring failure in a trailer outrigger mount.
How
High-fidelity SolidWorks FEA; custom material library matching Trail King's steel grade; ASME/SAE standards research.
Result
Identified two critical flaws — insufficient plate thickness and sub-standard bolt-hole edge distance. Redesign eliminated the failure mode.
Key Principle Applied

Bolt-hole tear-out occurs when edge distance falls below 1.5×–2× bolt diameter. The original geometry violated this threshold, concentrating shear stress under lateral loads.
